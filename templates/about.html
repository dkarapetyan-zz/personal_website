David Karapetyan was born and raised in Los Angeles, California and fell in love with music there when 12 years old, sleeping with his radio tuned to the classical station. He soon after began playing the piano and composing small classical pieces, a eventually branching out into jazz, rock, and hip-hop. While earning dual degrees in mathematics and English Literature from the University of California, Berkeley, and a Ph.D in mathematics from the University of Notre Dame, he was involved with the local music scenes in San Francisco and Chicago, composing and DJing a wide variety of music, including classical, jazz, hip-hop, EDM, salsa, merengue, bachata, cumbia, and argentine tango music.
